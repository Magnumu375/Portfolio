Who I am

Designer & developer based out of NYC

A self-taught web developer and front-end enthusiast with a passion for crafting visually engaging and highly interactive digital experiences. Starting from the ground up, the journey began with mastering HTML and CSS, refining skills through hands-on projects and continuous learning. With a keen eye for design and a love for clean, efficient code, curiosity transformed into expertise, leading to proficiency in front-end technologies and modern development techniques. From experimenting with simple layouts to building complex, dynamic interfaces, every step has been driven by creativity and the pursuit of perfection.

Over time, skills have evolved beyond just writing code—embracing the art of user experience, responsive design, and animation to bring websites to life. This growth led to the creation of Pixel and Logic Studio, a venture dedicated to web development and design solutions. By combining technical proficiency with a designer’s perspective, the goal is to push boundaries, integrating bold aesthetics with seamless functionality. With a passion for innovation and an ever-growing skill set, the focus remains on creating impactful digital experiences that stand out in today’s fast-paced web landscape.
